Email & Message Sync 🛡️

Extracts event details from emails and messages, syncing them to Google Calendar for easy confirmation.

Smart Scheduling with Friends 🚀

Shares calendar availability with friends to suggest optimal meeting times, eliminating the need for tools like When2Meet.

Recurring Event Recognition 📈

Detects recurring event patterns (e.g., "let's meet every Friday") and automatically adds them to the calendar.

Hidden Events 🦉

Allows users to hide events from shared calendars instead of deleting them.

Casual to Confirmed Plans ✨

Turns vague discussions into actual plans by prompting users to finalize missing details.

Auto-Cancel and Reschedule 🧰

Automatically updates the calendar when events are canceled or rescheduled via email or text.
